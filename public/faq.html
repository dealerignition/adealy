Dealy FAQ's

You have questions - We have answers


Why should I use Dealy?

Scheduling and distributing deals of the day has traditionally taken quite a bit of time. With dealy, you can set up once a month as many deals that you want. Then each day your new deal is distributed via email, social media, your website, etc.

1. Grow your sales
2. Increase traffic
3. Remarket to customers

Who is Dealy designed for?

Any e-commerce website can use Dealy for customer loyalty, increasing web traffic and growing their viral marketing.

How long does it take to set up Dealy?

Dealy is designed to be simple and fast to set up. In less than an hour you can be up and running. The two main parts of the set up process are:

1. Getting the Dealy ad code installed on to your site.
2. Adding your social media account information.

Can Dealy build a daily deal site?

Are you looking for a company to make your own deal of the day site? Yes? Well then Dealy is not for you.

Dealy is a secondary platform that works with existing e-commerce stores. Dealy allows e-commerce stores to have their own deal of the day system. We don't build daily deal websites. Sorry. Keep looking!

Do I have to manage my email list?

You only have to add new customers to the list. Dealy manages the customers who unsubscribe or bounce on your mailings.

Can I schedule multiple deals at one time?

Yes, we want you to schedule deals and then let dealy automate and distribute your deals each day.

What do I have to do to my website?

Add the Dealy ad code on the page(s) you want and your done. Yes, really. Finished. Our code has run on thousands of websites, and is simple to add and works with any site.

What is a threshold deal?

Threshold Deals - are deals that are triggered when so many people pruchase your deal. Common sites like Groupon and Living Social deals are typically triggered/threshold deals.

Dealy does not currently support threshold deals due to current limitations. Many e-commerce platforms do not provide us the connection/data feed to enable a trigger.Credit Card companies adn merchant banks are very sensitive to charging customers prior to shipping products. So we currently could NOT offer threshold deals for longer then one day.

How long have you been here?
